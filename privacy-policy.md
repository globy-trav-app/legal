This Privacy Policy describes how **Globy** ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and safeguards information in the Globy iOS app.

## Information We Collect
- **Account & Purchases.** Data associated with App Store purchases (handled by Apple).
- **Subscriptions & Analytics.** We use Adapty to process in-app purchases and subscription analytics.
- **Crash & Usage.** Aggregated diagnostic and usage data to improve stability and features.

## How We Use Information
- Provide core features of the app.
- Process subscriptions and restore purchases.
- Improve the app via aggregated analytics and diagnostics.
- Communicate important updates.

## Data Sharing
- **Apple** (App Store, in-app purchases).
- **Adapty** (subscription management & analytics).  
  Data is shared only as necessary to provide these services.

We do **not** sell personal data.

## Data Retention
We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law.
